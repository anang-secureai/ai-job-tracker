Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

What We Collect

This site uses the following third-party services to understand how visitors interact with the tracker and to measure the effectiveness of outreach efforts:

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4) — collects anonymized usage data such as pages visited, time on site, and general geographic region.
  • Google Ads — measures conversions from ad campaigns to understand which outreach efforts drive awareness.
  • Meta (Facebook) Pixel — tracks visits from Meta platforms to measure campaign effectiveness.
  • X (Twitter) Pixel — tracks visits from X to measure campaign effectiveness.

Cookies

These services use cookies — small text files stored in your browser — for analytics and ad measurement purposes. Cookies help us understand aggregate traffic patterns and campaign performance. We do not use cookies to identify you personally.

Consent

When you first visit this site, a consent banner appears at the bottom of the page. You can choose to:

  • Accept — analytics and advertising cookies are enabled.
  • Decline — no analytics or advertising cookies are set. Only essential functionality is preserved.

Your choice is stored in a cookie (cookie_consent) for one year so the banner does not reappear. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your cookies and revisiting the site.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Aggregated, anonymized analytics data is processed by the third-party services listed above under their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Meta Privacy Policy
  • X Privacy Policy
